Who is a Seeker ?

A seeker means - the one who is not ready to fall for a belief, the one who wants to know. The one who wants to know, in the journey of seeking, will eventually enhance he's capabilities - capability to be, capability to think, capability to do. Seeking is for all those who don't want mediocrity as a part of their life in any way. The one who is not a mediocre or a simpleton will have questions and find it's ways to grow.

What is Mystical Seeker ?

Mystical Seeker is an effort in this direction by 'Parth Chopra'. Its a non-profitable, volunteer-run community of seekers who are willing to transform their life.
