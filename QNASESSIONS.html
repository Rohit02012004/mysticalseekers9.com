QNA SESSIONS

    Q. What holds people back from doing what they want to do?

    Ans- Nothing. It is just that they don't want it bad enough.

    Q. How does one gets absolutely involved in what he is doing? U mentioned that your personality is fluid and you change it accordingly as needed, how do you do this??

    Ans- If you are not entangled in your head, naturally you'll be involved. But your head can't shut up because you think you are too smart. If you don't think too much of yourself, you'll naturally shut up and you'll be naturally Involved.

    Q. How does one gets absolutely involved in what he is doing? U mentioned that your personality is fluid and you change it accordingly as needed, how do you do this??

    Ans - In my childhood, I realized that "I am not my personality". Because I could easily change my Mindset, Personality, Voice... Because I just kept silent and looked, I learnt so many personalities. Once you learn deeply, you can change your Mindset, Personality... That's one way to truly realize that you are not your personality. But if you do "Personality Development", then you'll be a stone of a personality and you'll keep yourself in an illusion that you are your personality.

    Q. Bhaiya, can you please tell me the difference between desperation and extremely strong desire!?

    Ans-If you are desperate, you can't desire strong. Because to get to the stage where your desire could be really strong, you must be stable, patient and calm within you. Those who are not stable become Desperate. Those who are extremely stable can Desire Strongly.

    Q. How to get out of thoughts related to myself?

    Q. Do I really need to follow someone?

    Q.Osho said, there is nothing to do, nowhere to go, all goals without exeption are useless. Can you elaborate this pls? But the nature of life is evolution, always expanding.As sadhguru says, strive to go beyond physical. But it is said then the success of sadhana is to realise there is nowhere to go. Im confused!

    Q. Bhaiya what's your vision , why you're giving us these amazing things for free??
